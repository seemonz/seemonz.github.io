Climbing in Bishop

Went on a nine day climbing trip down to Bishop California with a bunch of climbing folk from Winnipeg.

I’ve never been specifically on a bouldering trip but turns out that just climbing with pads is pretty rad.

On the way back we took slight detour and checked out the Moab, UT area.

We got there at 3am and slept under the stairs. I woke up first.
